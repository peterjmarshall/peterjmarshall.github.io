About My Research

I have a few lines of research and theorizing:

  • The development of body maps in the infant brain.
  • Human-robot interaction, including how children perceive and engage with social robots.
  • A framework for moving towards a biologically coherent understanding of brain development.

My work has been funded by grants from the National Science Foundation, National Institutes of Health, and the James S. McDonnell Foundation.

Open Educational Resources
