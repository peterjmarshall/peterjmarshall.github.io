Peter J. Marshall

I am a developmental psychologist with a background in biology. This site is home to an ongoing project: building open materials that connect developmental biology, psychology, and neuroscience into a more coherent account of what it means to be a living, thinking, developing being.

Standard accounts in psychology and neuroscience tend to rely on a simplified picture of biology. But the actual biology is considerably more interesting than what students are typically exposed to.
